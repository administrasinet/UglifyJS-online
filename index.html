About JSMinify

JSMinify is an excellent tool to help you minify your JavaScript!

JSMinify is an online JavaScript compressor that allows you to compress and minify all of your JS files by up to 75% of their original size. Copy and paste your code and then compress.

Why You Should Compress JavaScript
